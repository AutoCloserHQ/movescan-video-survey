MoveScan Video Survey

Upload short room videos for your moving estimate

Record one short video per room. Start with a wide view, stand back as much as possible, use good lighting, and slowly scan from one side of the room to the other for 10–15 seconds.

Recording Tips
  • Record one room at a time.
  • Start by showing the full room from a corner or doorway.
  • Move slowly from one side of the room to the other.
  • Show large furniture, boxes, closets, shelves, and storage areas.
  • Keep each video around 10–15 seconds.
  • Wait for each video to fully finish uploading before pressing Submit.
